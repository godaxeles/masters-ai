### Account Management

Password Reset:
1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot Password" 
3. Enter email address
4. Check email for instructions

### Troubleshooting

Login Issues:
- Forgot password: Use "Forgot Password" link
- Account locked: Wait 15 minutes or contact support

Performance Issues:
- Slow system: Clear browser cache
- Slow reports: Reduce date range

### FAQ

Q: How do I add users?
A: Go to Administration > User Management > Add User.

Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: Yes, available for iOS and Android.
